Good morning, my Dearest Haozhen,

It's double seven festival today. On this very special day, I want you to know how much I like you, in a way I've never been before. I bet you do feel the same way.

“Life was like a box of chocolates, you never know what you're gonna get.” (You know where this quote come from, right?) I did not expect anything to happen the other night, when I lied in my bed, casually browsing Wechat moments. I saw the shared article from Yuanzhen and I just opened it for no reason. Typically I don't do that. But I did that night. The article itself made little sense to me. But when I scrolled down, I saw this interesting self introduction from you. "Hmmm, this is exactly the girl I’ve been looking for.” Scrolled a little bit more, I read your expectations, I was like “Wow, that's just me! Well, that’s me except the constellation thing…” So without any hesitation, I followed the Ice Cream Hutong, got the photos and your Wechat ID. Before you even realized, I was there saying hi and asking you out.

There are many stories during the entire life I have lived, and you have heard some of those. However, I believe this story is, by far, the best one. Probably I should add Arsenal in the footnote, which makes the story even better! That’s the dream! I’m sure there will be more better stories with you in the future.

I had never believed “Love at first sight”, “Made for each other”, or anything like that. But it seems like it is happening to me. For the last 4 weeks, I’ve been living in bliss. I wish I could share everything in my life with you, and I want to know everything happened and is happening to you.

I’ll absolutely visit you in London this Christmas, and we may even travel to Canary Islands together if you wish.

Have a great day!

Love,
Zechen

PS: I am aware that we just know each other for less than a month, we have just met twice, and now we are an ocean apart. I’m not sure if it’s proper to ask for a relationship under this situation. As far as I know, long distance relationship is hard, way much harder than you can imagine if you have not experienced it. But I’ll be DAMNED if I EVER let you go. No matter how hard road ahead gets, things will work out and nothing can be an obstacle. What do you say?
